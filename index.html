    Nasr Leads Porsche 1–2 At 2025 Sebring 12h

    03/15/2025

    The #7 Porsche Penske 963 is two-for two in the 2025 IMSA WeatherTech Sportscar Championship after winning today at the Mobil 1 Twelve Hours of Sebring. I'm overjoyed to see my favourite car win again and for Porsche to be running so strong here in North America.

    My favourite GTD Pro car—the #77 AO Racing Porsche 911 known affectionately as Rexy—crossed the line first in it's class. I'm overjoyed to see that team doing well, too. They're so young, yet they've found so much success in their two years; I'm hoping this is the beginning of another championship charge for both teams.

    Microsoft Publisher to be Discontined in 2026

    03/01/2025

    Microsoft has announced to Office 365 subscribers yesterday that Publisher will no longer be supported after October of 2026. While not many people (if any) really use Publisher anymore, I'm finding myself a little misty-eyed at the announcement.

    When I was a kid, I played around with Publisher 2003 and made my own websites. You could say that it's only becuase of Publisher that this site exists right now, since that program was (oddly) my entry into web development.

    You never really did live up to your potential, did you Publisher? But hey, fulfillment of potential is just an achievement in societal perception, and it's got nothing to do with personal fulfillment. RIP Publisher (1991-2026)—you'll be remembered, occasionally!

    Ferrari Finish 1-2-3 in Qatar

    02/28/2025

    At the 1812km of Qatar, Ferrari made history by sweeping the podium with their 499p hypercars. Antonio Fuoco in the #50 car was followed across the line by Robert Kubica in the privateer #83 and Alessandro Pier Guidi in the #51.

    This was such a special thing to watch, and I'm so excited for the rest of the World Endurance Championship season. This is truly a platinum era for sportscar racing, and sportscar racing is truly the pinnacle of motorsport. Forza Ferrari!
