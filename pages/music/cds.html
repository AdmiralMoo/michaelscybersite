Compact Discs

This is my CD collection. I've been collecting CDs since 2019; it's something I've always dreamed I would have, and I'm so happy that I've got all of these albums. The grand majority of these are from thrift stores; very few were bought new. As you can imagine, I love music, and this is my favourite way to listen to it. The CD—I think—is the perfect medium for listening to music: the packaging is durable and easily replaceable; the discs are indestructible when handled correctly; they put you in the studio with the artist when you've got a good recording and master; they don't wear out over time. I could go on and on about why I love the compact disc, and I reckon I will on this site someday.

The first CD's I bought were Green Day/Dookie, Nirvana/In Utero and Paramore/All We Know Is Falling, all of which were found at a thrift store.
