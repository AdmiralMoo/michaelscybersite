Albums For Rainy Days

Photo: MichaelsCybersite.net, 2025

Imagine: you're in a coffee shop in the mid to late 90's and they're playing some pretty harmless, inoffensive music with a couple of sparse hits in between. Lots of Jangle-Pop and Power-Pop mixed in with the Grunge/Post-Grunge of the day. Sometimes they stand out, sometimes they're just filler

This is one of my favourite playlists to have on in the rare occasion I'm not in the mood for a full album. I use the term "coffee shop rock" to describe the kind of inoffensive background music in between the hits.
