    • It's hard to pick favourites! I don't admit things to my list of all time favourite things very often, so the things on this page are pretty rock solid. It's folly to hum, haw and deliberate what is and isn't a favourite when I love such a huge swath of music; I listen to everything in my library, and although I might not be super into some of it at all times, I always come back to things. On this page, you'll find the bands I always go back to, the albums I always go back to and the songs that mean a lot to me.

    To see my favourite albums right now, check out the "Most Listened Music" section to see what I listened to most (updated monthly).
