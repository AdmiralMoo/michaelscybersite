My Favourite Books of 2025

Photo: MichaelsCybersite.net, 2025

2025 was a pretty big year for reading—after a long time of not being able to read after a brain injury, I was back to reading full-time. From fifty-two books, these were the books I enjoyed the most this year

A Farewell To Arms

by Earnest Hemingway

I think A Farewell to Arms is one of the greatest books of all time. It reeks with the stench of bloody, horrific memories; I was filled with a sense of hope and dread the whole way through. I don't know how much of it was real, but I feel like there was a Rinaldi and a Catherine somewhere in real life—they feel too much like memories to be just characters in a book. It's well written and perfectly paced.
