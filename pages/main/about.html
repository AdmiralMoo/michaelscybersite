    About This Site

    I started this site in September of 2024 to learn CSS and HTML. I've been interested in making my own website since I played around with the web builder in Microsoft Publisher 2003, but I haven't gotten around to learning it for real until now.

    I'm trying to grow this into a platform where I can show off my creations, store my lists and compendiums. I'm very passionate about the things I'm interested in, so this is a great way to indulge those interests further.

    About Me

    Me

    My name is Michael, and I'm a college student studying programming in Canada. Most of the time, you'll find me listening to music or quoting The Terminator in an Austrian accent. I enjoy learning about things and messing around with them. I read books, on occasion, and I take photos sometimes too; those things have their own page on this site, so I won't say much more about them.

    Cycling

    When it's above eight degrees, I enjoy cycling; when it's not, I think about cycling. It's a vicious cycle, really.

    Computer-Based Activities

    On the computer, I do a lot of programming in Python and C#, though I also know C++, Visual Basic, a bit of JavaScript and—hence the website you're reading this on—I'm learning HTML and CSS. I've done a lot of 3D Modelling in Blender, a fair bit of special-effects and video editing in Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro, and some audio editing in Adobe Audition; I've picked up all sorts of skills getting into things here and there.

    Games

    I used to play a lot of games, but not so much anymore. On the computer, my all-time favourite games have always been SimCity 4 (2003), Zoo Tycoon 2 (2004) and Microsoft Flight Simulator 2004, and I still play a lot of those. In terms of modern games, I've played a decent amount of Automobilista 2 (2020) and Assetto Corsa (2014). The only console I really play is the PlayStation 2; MVP Baseball 2005 (2005), Gran Turismo 3 (2001) and ATV Offroad Fury 2 (2002) are the games I play most often on there.

    Movies

    My favourite movie is The Terminator (1984), while Star Wars (1977) and Jurassic Park (1993) follow closely behind. Independence Day (1996), 2001: A Space Odyssyey (1968), Top Gun (1986), The Outlaw Josey Wales (1971), and The Transformers Movie (1986) make it into the Top 10.

    I don't watch movies very often, but when I do, it's usually The Terminator.

    TV Shows

    I've watched a lot of Star Trek—the entirety of TOS (three times), TNG, Voyager (twice), DS9 and Enterprise. It sounds ridiculous that I've watched TOS thrice, but I was introduced to it by my dad—as with a lot of my interests—when I was very young, so I've had time.

    Other than that, I'm a big fan of The Transformers (1984-1987).

    I don't watch much TV anymore, really. I guess that goes for all my screen-based hobbies: I don't do much of them anymore unless I'm creating something.

    BBC's Walking With Dinosaurs (1999) is hands down my all time favourite documentary.

    Racing

    What I do watch a lot of is racing: I've been a fan of Formula 1 for quite some time, but my real interest is sporstscar endurance racing in series such as IMSA and WEC. Watching cars go in circles for twelve hours is a truly wonderful thing.
