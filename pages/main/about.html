    About This Site

    I started this site in September of 2024 to learn CSS and HTML. I've been interested in making my own website since I played around with the web builder in Microsoft Publisher 2003, but I haven't gotten around to learning it for real until now.

    I'm trying to grow this into a platform where I can show off my creations, store my lists and compendiums. I'm very passionate about the things I'm interested in, so this is a great way to indulge those interests further. This is the ultimate ongoing project!

    Racing

    I watch a lot of racing! Watching people drive fast cars in overcomplicated circles for 12 hours sounds like a good time to me.

    IMSA & WEC

    I've always been fascinated by the spaceships of La Sarthe playing Gran Turismo 3 and 4 as a kid. Sportscar endurance racing—specifically IMSA—is my favourite thing to watch. I've been to a couple of IMSA races and it's the most exhilirating thing!

    I would love to go to Le Mans someday—that would be my dream race. Seeing Ferrari return to the top class of motor racing and win Le Mans three consecutive years in a row has been one of the most special things I've seen in sport. Every IMSA or WEC race is packed with nonstop action and fairy-tale-like stories are written. To me, this is the ultimate form of motorsport—where drivers of all disciplines come together to duke it out on an even playing field.

    Favourite Drivers: Felipe Nasr, Antonio Fuoco, Dane Cameron, Martin Brundle, Robert Kubica, Kevin Estre, Tom Kristensen, Fernando Alonso, Laurin Heinrich

    Favourite Era: LMDh/Hypercar, GT-One

    My Dad has always liked Ferrari, and so have I. Schumacher was everywhere when I was younger—in movies, books, a photo on the wall of the kindergarten classroom. Alonso's golden years at Ferrari in the early 2010's was the first time I was really into F1. There was a lull in the late 2010's for me, but I came back to it not too long after. I watch every competitive session every weekend these days

Oscar Piastri has been a favourite recently as well—I thought he was awesome in F2, and I'm so impressed by him in F1 thus far. I'm hoping for a World Championship this year!

    Baseball

    Along with racing, I also enjoy watching some baseball from time to time.

    I've always been a Blue Jays fan. I have very fond memories of going to the super-cheap games sitting up in the 500's level at the Rogers Centre in the late 2000's and early 2010's. I've been to quite a few games, though I've regrettably not seen one in a couple of years since I moved away.

    Growing up, my favourite player was Roy Halladay—it was just amazing watching him on the mound! I was sad when he got traded, but he went on to even greater things. One of my favourite memories was when he made his first start in Toronto since the trade, and he received a standing ovation; we all clapped because he was Roy Halladay.

    Jose Bautista was an amazing story, too: an underdog who had a breakout season relatively late in his career, propelling him to stardom. It was so cool watching him hit and run the bases, and it still feels weird not having him guarding right field. Did you know that Bautista owns (owned?) a Booster Juice in downtown Toronto?

    The current Blue Jays team is very exciting and I'm hoping they'll do something with the 2025 post-season.

    Favourite Team: Toronto Blue Jays
    Favourite Players: Roy Halladay, Jose Bautista
    Favourite Game I've Been To: David Price's Toronto debut
