    All-Time Favourites

    It's hard to pick favourites! I'm not quite sure what my all-time favourite are, but my list of favourite bands is pretty much set in stone. That list has grown slowly but surely, and I know those bands aren't going anywhere. It's much easier to pick out my favourite classical works than it is albums as a whole. For the following list, I've chosen classical releases, rather than works or compositions specifically. I love a lot of albums, and The Dark Side of The Moon is the only one I can confidently put on a list like this.

    My Favourite bands:

    • Green Day
    • Alice In Chains
    • The Smashing Pumpkins
    • Nine Inch Nails
    • Pink Floyd

    My Favourite Classical Releases:

    • Richard Strauss/Also Sprach Zarathustra
    • Antonín Dvořák/Symphony No. 9
    • Gustav Holst/The Planets
    • Johann Strauss II/An Der Schonen, Blauen Donau
    • Georges Bizet/Carmen Suites Nos. 1 & 2

    My Favourite Albums:

    • Pink Floyd/The Dark Side of The Moon
